About

In SIQA you can ask and answer questions, comment and vote for the questions of others and their answers. Questions can be tagged with the relevant keywords. Our site is moderated by our members.

Users can earn points based on their questions and answers, and votes they gather. These points are added to the users account. No points are necessary to ask or answer the questions, so please join us!

Points Calculation

  • For each approved question and answer by our moderator you earn 5 points respectively.
  • For accepted answers you earn 10 points.
  • Total points will be the sum of above 2 and total votes you gather in your questions and answers.
